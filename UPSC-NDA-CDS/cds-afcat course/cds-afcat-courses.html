The CDS Stands for Combined Defence Service Examination. This Exam is conducted for the selection of the Officers into the various Armed forces academies to be an officer. One can join the following Armed force academies through the CDS:

  • Indian Air Force Academy
  • Indian Military Academy
  • Indian Naval Academy
  • Officer’s Training Academy etc

Required Eligibility Criteria For CDS:

The Education Qualification and the age limit is required are different for each force like AFA, IMA, INA and OTA, etc. Here are the details:

  • A candidate must be an Indian Candidate must be unmarried.
  • For the OTA, Divorced females can also apply for OTA.
  • All the candidates who are in their final year or semester can also apply for the vacancy.
  • There is no relaxation for anyone in any category, it will be the same for everyone.
