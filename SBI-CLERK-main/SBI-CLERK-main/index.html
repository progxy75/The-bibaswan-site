Steps to Apply for SBI Clerk Recruitment 2023

Follow the instructions below to apply for SBI Clerk 2023:

1. Go to the official website of SBI, which is www.sbi.co.in.
2. Provide your necessary credentials during the registration process.
3. Upon successful registration, the system will generate a provisional registration number and password. It is advised to save these details for future use.
4. Refer to the notification for guidelines and upload scanned copies of your photograph and signature accordingly.
5. Enter your educational and other relevant details.
6. Preview and verify the entire application by clicking on the preview tab before submitting it finally.
7. After verification, click on the final submit button. Proceed to the payment tab for the application fee.
8. After successfully paying the application fee, candidates will receive a mail or message on their registered email ID/phone number, and this will signify the completion of the application process.
9. Save the application form or take a printout for future reference.

The SBI Clerk Exam 2023 is divided into two stages, namely Prelims and Mains, and what sets it apart is the omission of an interview. SBI Clerk 2023 Selection Process

The criteria Preliminary and Mains are the two stages of SBI Clerk selection criteria.

1. Online Preliminary Examination: SBI will select a specific number of candidates from different categories based on the requirements for the Online Main Examination.

2. Online Mains Examination: To be considered eligible for the Final Selection, candidates must achieve passing marks or higher in each section of the main exam and meet the overall cut-off.

Final Result: The Final result is determined through a merit list in the SBI Clerk Mains Exam.
SBI facilitates candidate allotment based on the preferences indicated during registration. SBI plays a crucial role in provisional allotment, and information regarding the exam date, joining dates, and other formalities will be provided soon.
