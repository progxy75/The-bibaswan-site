Kolkata Police SI Recruitment Exam 2023

The West Bengal Police Recruitment Board (WBPRB) conducts the Kolkata Police SI Recruitment Examination for hiring Sub Inspectors in the Kolkata Police. You need to meet certain criteria in order to apply for this examination. First, one  should have to be between the ages of 20 and 27 years and  should have completed his or her graduation.  Keep an eye out for the Kolkata Police SI Recruitment 2023 Notification, which will be released soon.Visit the official website of WBPRB in order to apply for the Kolkata police SI Recruitment Examination 2023. 

Kolkata Police SI Recruitment Exam 2023: Notification

The Kolkata Police is going to recruit Sub Inspectors (SI) for the year 2023. They will soon release an official notification regarding Kolkata Police SI recruitment.  The online application process will start soon, but the deadline to apply has not yet been announced. It is recommended to regularly visit the official website of the WBPRB  for any updates on the recruitment process.

The selection process for Kolkata Police SI includes several stages. Firstly, there is a written examination where candidates are tested based on their knowledge and skills. Then, there is a physical measurement test to ensure that candidates meet the required physical standards. After that, there is a physical efficiency test to assess candidates’ physical fitness and endurance.

Once candidates clear these tests, they proceed to the Final Competitive Written exam, which is a comprehensive assessment of their knowledge and abilities. Finally, there is an interview where candidates have the opportunity to showcase their personality, communication skills, and suitability for the SI position.

These multiple stages of the selection process are designed to ensure that the most suitable candidates are chosen for the Kolkata Police SI position.

Kolkata Police SI Recruitment Exam 2023: How to Apply

Aspirants who are eligible to apply for the recruitment of SI in Kolkata Police can submit their application form online or offline. Please find below both the procedures for a better understanding of how to submit an application:

Steps for Online Application Form Submission

  • Visit the West Bengal Police’s official website, http://wbpolice.gov.in.in
  • Go to the “Notice for Recruitment of Sub Inspector/ Sub-Inspectress (Unarmed Branch) and Sergeant in KP – 2023” link.
  • Download the notification and carefully study it.
  • Select the “Apply Online” option.
  • Fill out the application form with required information in the provided fields.
  • Upload scanned copies of your photos and signature.
  • Then pay the application fee online.
  • For the final submission of your application form, click the “Submit” button.
  • A copy of your Application Form will display on the screen, with your Image, Signature, and Unique Application Serial Number.
  • Download the form and keep it for future reference

 

Steps for Offline Application Form Submission

You will have to follow all the previous steps mentioned above and complete the Application Form. After that take a printout and mail the Application Form to the following address: To the Chairman, West Bengal Police Recruitment Board, Araksha Bhaban, 5th Floor, 6th Cross Road, Block – DJ, Sector-II, Salt Lake City, Kolkata – 700 091.

Kolkata Police SI Recruitment Exam 2023: Selection Process

 The Kolkata Police SI Recruitment 2023 follows a multi-stage selection process. Here are the stages involved:

  1. Written Examination: Candidates will have to appear for a written examination where their knowledge and skills will be assessed.
  2. Physical Measurement Test (PMT): Candidates who qualify in the written examination will undergo a physical measurement test to ensure they meet the required physical standards.
  3. Physical Efficiency Test (PET): Candidates who clear the physical measurement test will participate in a physical efficiency test, which evaluates their physical fitness and endurance.
  4. Final Combined Competitive Exam: Candidates who pass the PET will move on to the Final Combined Competitive Exam, which is a comprehensive assessment of their knowledge and abilities.
  5. Personality Test: Shortlisted candidates from the Final Combined Competitive Exam will undergo a personality test or interview to evaluate their suitability for the SI position.

The final selection will be based on the candidate’s performance in all stages of the selection process. It is important for candidates to perform well in each stage to increase their chances of being selected as Sub Inspector in Kolkata Police.

Kolkata Police SI Recruitment Exam 2023: Admit Card

The Kolkata Police SI Admit Card is an important document that candidates need to download from the official website of WBPRB and carry along with them to the examination hall. Candidates must log in using their registration ID, which was provided during the online application process to download the Admit Card. 

The admit card contains essential information such as the examination venue, timing, and important instructions for the exam day. It serves as proof of eligibility to appear for the examination.

The link to download the admit card will be activated by the WBPRB. Candidates are advised to regularly check the official website of WBPRB for updates and notifications regarding the availability of the admit card.
