IBPS PO Educational Qualification:

There is an eligibility criteria for IBPS CRP Exam. If the student fit for those eligibility criteria, can only sit in the Exam.

Graduation Degree:

Candidate have to be graduate (BA, BCom, BSc, B. Tech or as such) from a recognized University. Only then he/she will be eligible for IBPS PO 2023 Exam. Make sure that candidates have a valid Mark-sheet / Degree Certificate that state that they are a graduate on the day they register for this exam.

Computer Literacy:

The IBPS PO 2023 Exam will be conducted in online mode, so it is compulsory that the candidate should have a good understanding of computer system, for the job as well.

Language Proficiency:

-The candidate must be able to read, write and speak the official language of the State/ UT. 

-Verbal and written proficiency in the official language of the State/UT is desirable

Nationality / Citizenship of Applicants:

A candidate applying for IBPS PO Examination must be one of the following:

  1. Citizen of India
  2. Subject of Nepal or Bhutan
  3. Tibetan Refugee who came to India before 1st January 1962 with the intention of permanent settlement
  4. Person of Indian Origin (PIO) who has migrated from Burma, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Vietnam or East African countries of Zaire, Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia, Ethiopia, Malawi, with the intention of permanent settlement in India

Candidates belonging to the category 2, 3, 4 must have a certificate of eligibility issued by the Government of India in their favor.

IBPS PO Recruitment 2023:

Common Recruitment Process (CRP) was started in 2011 is a process for recruiting officers and clerks in Indian bank. Every year Probationary Officers (PO) and Management Trainees (MT) are recruited through CRP, which is commonly known as the IBPS PO exam. There are three phases in total (preliminary, mains and interview) and the candidate have to clear each of them to proceed to the next phase. Following are the important steps of the IBPS PO Recruitment Process:

  • Online Preliminary Examination:

An adequate number of candidates in each category are shortlisted by IBPS depending upon marks  for Online Main Examination

  • Online Mains Examination:

Candidates must qualify this section as well  to be eligible for the interview.

  • Interview:

The interview will be conducted by the Participating Organizations and coordinated by the Nodal Bank in each State/ UT with the help of IBPS

Final Result:

The Final result of IBPS PO 2023 will be declared on the basis IBPS PO Mains and Interview Rounds. Selected Candidates shall be cons for appointment subject to Medical Fitness.

  • Provisional Allotment:

The provisional allotment is a type of document that contain the name of IBPS PO candidate who have been provisionally allotted to the bank. IBPS will release the provisional allotment of selected candidate. According to candidates preference, the IBPS Banks are allotted. After the Provisional allotment, the selection procedure lies with the respective banks. Those respective banks will inform the candidates about the joining dates and procedures.

IBPS PO 2023 Exam Pattern:

Probationary Officers will be hired on the basis of online exams,  Tier 1 or IBPS Preliminary Exam and Tier 2 or IBPS Mains Exam and an Interview round. 

IBPS PO Prelims Exam Pattern 2023:

There will be separate sectional timing for each section in the preliminary exam

The minimum cut-off marks will be decided by IBPS. Candidates have to clear in each of the three tests by getting minimum cut-off marks.

Following are some important points for IBPS PO Prelims Exam Pattern 2023:

 

Total Section: 3

Duration of IBPS PO Prelims Exam:1 hour and 20 minutes for each section

Mode of Examination: Online

Total Number of Question: 100

Maximum score: 100 Marks

Marks deducted for each wrong answer: 0.25

 

The IBPO PO Main:

-It has 5(4+1) sections

-Total score of 200+25 marks

-Total duration of 180+30 minutes

-Mode of Exam-Online.

IBPS will be conducting a descriptive paper in its mains exam for the post of Probationary Officer to selection of candidates It is necessary to qualify both preliminary and mains examinations. Then only he/she will be eligible for the interview process.

Penalty for Wrong Answers:

One fourth marks will be deducted for each wrong answers marked in both Preliminary and Mains exams. No penalty for leaving a question blank.

 

IBPS PO Interview

Qualified candidate of the mains exam are called for a face to face interview.

Generally, interviewer ask questions to the candidates about themselves, banking sector, current affairs, general awareness, etc.

 

Things should be considered:

-The candidates must be well dressed,

-Candidate have to be confident and Calm.

-Dont forget to carry all required documents to the interview.

Note:

Maximum marks: 100

The minimum qualifying mark in the interview: 40% (35% for SC/ST/OBC/PWD candidates).

Duration on interview: generally, 15-20 minutes long

Final Score Calculation for IBPS PO Exam:

The final score for the IBPS PO exam is calculated on the basis of:

  • Marks obtained in the Preliminary Exam (Tier-1) will not be considered in the final score and are only for qualifying to Mains Exam (Tier-2).
  • Candidates must qualify for Phase-2 and Interview separately to be eligible for final merit.
  • The weightage (ratio) of IBPS PO Main exam and interview for the final merit list will be 80:20 respectively.
  • The aggregate score out of 100 is used for the final merit list for each category. Candidates with top merit rank in each category are finally selected.

IBPS exam syllabus is almost similar to any other bank exam. The IBPS PO syllabus is divided into two parts:

IBPS PO Prelims Exam Syllabus 2023

IBPS PO Mains Exam Syllabus 2023

It consists 5 sections: Reasoning, English Language, Quantitative, Aptitude, General Awareness, Computer Aptitude.

IBPS PO Prelims Exam Syllabus 2023 comprises of Reasoning Ability, Quantitative Aptitude and English Language.

There will be three sections for the IBPS PO Prelims Exam: English, Quantitative Aptitude, and Reasoning. The question paper will carry 30 marks for English, 35 marks for Quantitative Aptitude, and 35 marks for Reasoning. Preliminary Examination will last 60 minutes.

Reasoning, Economy, English, Data Interpretation, and Letter & Essay writing are the subjects for IBPS PO Main Exam. The question paper will carry 60 marks for reasoning, 40 marks for economics, 40 marks for English, 60 marks for data interpretation, and 25 marks for English language (essays and letters). 225 is the total marks for the exam and 180 minutes will be the duration.
