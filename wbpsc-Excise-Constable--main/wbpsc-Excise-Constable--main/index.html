WB Excise Constable Recruitment 2023: Notification

WB Excise Constable 2023 notification will be released soon on the official website of the WBPRB. Candidates should be between 18 and 27 years of age  and have qualified the Madyamik Examination from the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education or equivalent in order to sit for the WB Excise Constable examination. Additionally, he or she must have Bengali or Nepali as a language in their 10th standard examination.

WB Excise Constable Recruitment 2023: Apply Online

Candidates can apply when the application dates will be announced by the authorities. They can follow these simple steps to complete the application process:

WB Excise Constable Recruitment Examination 2023: Selection Process

The board will shortlist the best applicants through a selection process. Candidates must clear all the stages for getting the job.The selection process is divided into the following parts, as defined by the committee for the year 2019. The same stages are expected to be followed for the 2023 cycle. The stages are as follows:

WB Excise Constable Recruitment Examination 2023: Admit Card

Candidates should download their admit card prior to the exam. They can get their admit cards by visiting the official website of the WBPRB. The following are the processes for downloading the admit card:

  • Visit the official website.
  • On the webpage, click the ‘Recruitment’ button.
  • Look for the announcement titled ‘WB Excise Admit Card’ and click on it.
  • Enter your legitimate login information, such as your registration number and password.
  • When your admit card displays on the screen, take a print out of it. 

WB Excise Constable Recruitment Examination 2023: Result

WBPRB will publish the results on its official website. The cut-off marks will be given together with the candidates’ scores. The process to get the result are as follows:

  • Visit the official website.
  •  Click on the “Recruitment” button.
  • Search and select the “WB Excise Result” message.
  • The result will come out in a PDF format. You can fast access your result by hitting CTRL+F,
