RRB NTPC 2023 Eligibility

The RRB NTPC Eligibility will depend on different factors like Age limit, Nationality, and Education Qualification. Those who are fit for these eligibility criteria will only applicable for the next process.

The following is the condition that a candidate should fulfil to be eligible for RRB NTPC Nationality

Candidate should be:

  • Citizen of India, Nepal, or Bhutan.
  • Or a Tibetan refugee who came over to India before 1 January 1962 to permanently settle in India.

RRB NTPC 2023 Educational Qualification:

Railway Recruitment Board hires across various posts bifurcated into two separate sections with respect to the candidate’s educational qualification.

  • For 12th passed candidate: Trains Clerk/ Commercial Cum Ticket Clerk / Accounts Clerk Cum Typist / Junior Clerk Cum Typist / Junior Time Keeper
  • For Graduate candidates: Commercial Apprentice / Station Master / Goods Guard / Senior Clerk Cum Typist / Senior Time Keeper / Traffic Assistant.

RRB NTPC Age Limit

In order to apply for the RRB NTPC 2023 Exam, the candidates must fulfil the below criteria to be eligible for the exam and final recruitment.

  • A candidate should age between 18 to 30 years for undergraduate-level posts,
  • 18 years is the minimum age limit for graduate-level posts
  • 33 years is the upper age limit for graduate-level posts.
  • Age relaxation will be provided by the Board for reserved categories of candidates.

RRB NTPC 2023 Syllabus

The RRB NTPC Syllabus will be updated every year along with the recruitment notification. Candidate must go through the RRB NTPC syllabus which will help the candidate to clear the Exam.

For each tier in English and Hindi, the subject-wise syllabus are mentioned below:

General Awareness Syllabus: History, Geography, Economy, Indian Polity and Governance, General Science, Current Affairs, etc.
Mathematics Syllabus: Number System, Decimals, LCM, HCF, Ratio and Proportions, Time and Work, Mensuration, Percentage, Elementary Statistics, etc.

A better understanding of the syllabus helps candidates to identify the topic that they have to focus and they can manage their strong and weak topics smartly.

RRB NTPC Exam Pattern

Understanding exam patterns will help you clear the exam. According to the recent update, the National Recruitment Agency (NRA) to conduct a Common Eligibility Test (CET) for all non-gazetted posts (Group B and C) under the Railway Recruitment Board has been introduced by the union cabinet. The RRB NTPC exam consists of three stages: CBT 1, CBT 2, and Skill Test. Below, you can find the detailed RRB NTPC Exam Pattern

RRB CBT 1: It consists of 3 sections- General Intelligence and Reasoning, General Awareness, and Mathematics. It is compulsory to qualify CBT 1 Exam to appear for CBT 2 Exam. Read out the following information:

General Awareness section includes 40 questions.
Mathematics and Reasoning will comprise 30 questions.
Each question carries one mark
25 marks were deducted for each incorrect answer.
Candidate will have only 90 minutes to attempt the entire paper.

RRB CBT 2:  Both CBT 1 and CBT 2 have a similar exam pattern, only the level and number of questions are increased.

General Awareness section includes 50 questions.
Mathematics and Reasoning will comprise 35 questions.
One mark for each correct answer.
1/3rd mark deducted for each incorrect answer.
Candidate will have only 90 minutes to attempt the entire paper.

RRB NTPC 2023 Selection Process

There are three stages for RRB NTPC Selection Process. The three stages are CBT 1, CBT 2, and Skill Test. It is compulsory to clear all three stages for NTPC jobs.

Aspirants will be selected based on the marks scored in CBT 1 and CBT 2 whereas the third stage will be qualifying in nature. It is mandatory to pass all three stages, only then you will be eligible for the document verification and medical test.

NOTE:

For the post of Trains Clerk, Commercial cum Ticket Clerk, Goods Guard, Senior Commercial cum Ticket Clerk, or Commercial Apprentice, candidates do not have to appear for test sets test.

They will have to appear for two-stage CBT followed by document verification and medical examination.

RRB NTPC 2023 Salary

RRB NTPC Salary consists of multiple slabs for different posts. Basic salary of Rs. 18,000 per month for RRB NTPC Candidates along with additional allowances such as Dearness allowance (DA), Daily Allowance, Transport Allowance, House Rent Allowance (HRA), Allowance for Night Duty, Medical Facilities, and Overtime Allowance.

These allowances will further add to their monthly salary, making the in-hand salary for RRB NTPC posts

  • Salary for the undergraduate post: Range from Rs. 19,900 to Rs. 21,700 per month
  • Salary for graduate posts: Range from Rs. 25,500 to Rs. 35,400 per month.

RRB NTPC Mock Test:

As the RRB NTPC exam is conducted online, it is crucial to regularly take RRB NTPC mock tests to enhance your exam preparation. You have the option to either avail of our test series and receive a complimentary mock test or subscribe to gain unrestricted access to all mock tests

Advantage of solving RRB NTPC mock test

  • Assessing Exam Readiness: Mock tests provide an opportunity to gauge your readiness for the RRB NTPC exam, helping you identify areas that require improvement.
  • Time Management Practice: By attempting mock tests, you can practice managing your time effectively, ensuring that you complete the exam within the given time frame
  • Familiarity with Exam Interface: Mock tests simulate the actual exam interface, allowing you to become familiar with the online platform and its features, thereby reducing exam-day anxiety.
  • Identifying Strengths and Weaknesses: Through mock tests, you can identify your strengths and weaknesses in different sections, enabling you to focus on areas that need more attention during your preparation.
  • Improving Speed and Accuracy: Regularly solving mock tests enhances your speed and accuracy in answering questions, resulting in improved performance during the actual exam.

Consistent practice and attempting mock tests are essential for boosting your confidence and achieving success in the RRB NTPC 2023 exam.
