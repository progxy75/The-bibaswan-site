Kolkata Police Constable Recruitment 2023

WBPRB released a notification for the recruitment of Kolkata police constables and lady constables in 2023, wherein selection will be based on a written test. Last year, a notification for the Kolkata Police Constable Recruitment was published by the West Bengal Police Recruitment Board (WBPRB) on May 24th, 2022 on their official website.

Kolkata Police Constable Recruitment: Apply Online

Candidates can submit their applications for the Kolkata police constable position. Kindly follow the instructions outlined below:

Deadline of Application: 29th May to 27th June 2022.

  1. Candidates must access the official website of the West Bengal Police Recruitment Board in order to fill the online application form.
  2. Proceed by selecting the designated link and thoroughly review the detailed notification.
  3. Following that, candidates will be redirected to the login page.
  4. Complete all the required fields, including personal details like name, date of birth, email address, mobile number, father’s name, address, and educational qualifications.
  5. Lastly, submit the application form and retain a printed copy for future reference.

Kolkata Police Constable Recruitment 2023: Selection Process

The selection process has several stages, as outlined below:

  • Preliminary Written Test
  • Physical Measurement Test (PMT) and Physical Efficiency Test (PET)
  • Final Written Examination
  • Interview

These stages form an integral part of the selection procedure.

Kolkata Police Constable Recruitment 2023: Eligibility Criteria

Candidates aspiring to take part in the recruitment procedure must fulfil the specified requirements like age limits, educational qualifications, physical standards, and more. Please refer to the comprehensive information provided below for further details.

 The age criteria for the recruitment process are as follows

  1. Candidates must be in the age range of 18 to 27 years in order to apply for the recruitment process.
  2. Candidates belonging to Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, and Other Backward Classes may be eligible for relaxation in the upper age limit, as per government regulations.

Educational Background Requirement

The candidate should have successfully cleared the Madhyamik Examination conducted by the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education or an equivalent examination in order to be eligible for the recruitment process.
