RRB Group D Recruitment 2023: Application Process

The following are the steps to filling up your RRB Group D 2023 Online Application form:

  • Visit the regional Railway Recruitment Board’s official website.
  • Register yourself by providing your name, father’s and mother’s names, date of birth, Aadhar number, SSLC/Metric Registration number, year of graduation, mobile number, and email address, among other information.
  • The mobile number and email Id will be verified through a one-time password
  • Continue with the online application by clicking on “Already Registered” and entering your registration number and password that were produced automatically.
  • Now enter your educational details, any additional information, and preferences for the positions.
  • Bank information is also necessary to obtain a refund of the fees
  • Upload your scanned signature and passport-size photo in the appropriate format.
  • After entering all the necessary information, pay the relevant application cost using online or offline methods.

RRB Group D Recruitment Examination 2023: Syllabus

General Arithmetic Syllabus:

BODMAS, LCM and HCF, Ratio and Proportion, Percentages, Mensuration, Time and Work, Time and Distance, and Number Systems Profit and loss, simple and compound interest, Square Root, age calculations, calendar and clock, geometry, trigonometry, elementary statistics, pipes, and cistern

General Intelligence and Reasoning Syllabus:

Analogies, Alphabetical and Number Series, Coding and Decoding, Mathematical Operations, Relationships, Syllogism, Jumbling, Venn diagram, Data Interpretation and Sufficiency, Conclusions and Decision Making, Similarities and Differences, Analytical Reasoning, Classification, Directions, Statement-Arguments and Assumptions, and so on.

General Awareness Syllabus:

Current affairs, Indian geography, culture, and history, including the freedom movement, Indian polity and constitution, Indian economy, environmental issues affecting India and the rest of the world, sports, general scientific and technology developments, and so on.

RRB Group D Recruitment Examination 2023: Admit Card

Candidates who successfully applied for the RRB Group D 2023 examination will be given an admit card in two phases: computer-based test (CBT) and physical efficiency test (PET).

The PET admit card will be sent to those who passed the CBT for RRC Group D Level 1. Candidates need to log in to the official website of the Railway Recruitment Board using their Registration Number/Roll No. and Date of Birth/Password to download their admit card.

RRB Group D Recruitment Examination 2023: Result

The Railway Recruitment Board announce the results of the RRB Group D 2023 examination around a month after it is held. The CBT results will be approved in the first phase, and candidates who will qualify this exam will be eligible to appear for the RRC Group D 2023 PET exam.

Additionally, the PET result will be provided when the Board has successfully completed the Physical Ability Test for men and women. 

RRB Group D Recruitment Examination 2023: Cut Off

Candidates must obtain the minimum qualifying marks or cut-off marks in the RRB Group D 2023 Exam in order to be eligible for the Computer-based Test (CBT). The cut-off marks will be determined by the board and announced together with the results. The cut-off is determined by the following factors: 

  • The number of vacancies
  • The examination’s difficulty level 
  • The number of candidates appeared for the examination 
  • The process of normalisation
