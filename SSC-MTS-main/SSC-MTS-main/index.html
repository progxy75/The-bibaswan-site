SSC MTS Recruitment 2023 Exam

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) will be organizing the SSC MTS Recruitment 2023 Exam, a nationwide examination to select individuals for non-gazetted, non-ministerial posts in Group-C of the General Central Service. This examination opens the path to employment in various ministries, departments, and government offices in India. The selected candidates for multi-tasking staff positions will receive a salary at Pay Level-1 as per the 7th Pay Commission’s Pay Matrix, with a basic pay range of Rs. 5,200-20,200 plus a Grade Pay of Rs. 1,800.

SSC is also planning to conduct the SSC Multi-Tasking (Non-Technical) Staff and Havaldar (CBIC & CBN) Examination in 2023, offering job opportunities to individuals who have passed the 10th grade.

SSC MTS Exam 2023

Every year, the Staff Selection Commission conducts the SSC MTS (Multi-Tasking Staff Exam) in three distinct stages. SSC is organizing the SSC MTS 2023 Exam (Multitasking Staff) to hire individuals for various positions, including the role of:

Junior Gestetner Operator, Chowkidar, Safaiwala, Mali, Havaldar, Peon, Daftary, Jamadar,  etc.

SSC MTS Recruitment 2023 Notification

  • On the 30th of June 2023, the Staff Selection Commission (SSC) will release the SSC MTS Notification 2023, which will declare the openings for SSC MTS Havaldar (CBIC and CBN) & Multi-Tasking Staff.
  • The SSC MTS examination is conducted through a Computer Based Examination (Objective test)
  • It is a total of 270 marks.

The recently published SSC MTS notification includes crucial information such as the exam pattern, syllabus, dates, vacancy, eligibility criteria, and other important details.

SSC MTS 2023 Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for the SSC MTS 2023 Exam, candidates need to fulfil three key criteria. Before completing the applications for the SSC MTS Exam, candidates must be familiar with the essential Eligibility Criteria related to nationality, educational qualifications, and age restrictions. Get a glimpse of the criteria.

SSC MTS Citizenship:

The candidate has to fall under either of the categories:

  • A citizen of India.
  • The subject of Nepal
  • The subject of Bhutan.
  • Tibetan Refugee
  • Person who is an Indian but have migrated from Sri Lanka, Uganda, Zambia, Malawi, Zaire, Ethiopia, and Vietnam Pakistan, Burma, Afghanistan, Kenya, Tanzania.

SSC MTS Age Limit

The Recruitment Rules of various User Departments outline the age limits for the posts as follows:

  1. Candidates must be at least 18 years old and should not exceed 25 years old. They should be born between 02-01-1999 and 01-01-2006.
  2. For the age range of 18-27 years, candidates should be born between 02-01-1997 and 01-01-2006.
  3. In addition to the aforementioned Age Requirement, candidates belonging to reserved categories receive age relaxation as per Government Rules.
  4. Regarding the Havaldar position in CBIC and CBN, Department of Revenue, candidates must be at least 18 years old and not exceed 27 years old. They should be born between 02-01-1996 and 01-01-2006 to be eligible for applying.

SSC MTS Educational Qualification

To be eligible for the SSC MTS 2023 Exam, candidates must have completed and passed the Matriculation (10th class) examination or its equivalent from a recognized State Board, University, or Institute.

SSC MTS 2023 Online Application

Commencing in the third week of June 2023, the online registration for SSC MTS 2023 will initiate alongside the announcement of the SSC MTS Notification 2023. The notification will provide the deadline for submitting online applications for the SSC MTS 2023 exam.

The updated exam structure for SSC MTS Paper-I has been published together with the SSC MTS Notification 2023. The exam will be conducted in a computerized format and will be divided into two sessions. Both sessions are required to be attempted by candidates. In Session II, for each incorrect answer, there will be a deduction of 1 mark, while Session-I does not carry any negative marking. SSC MTS 2023 Salary

  • The Staff Selection Commission MTS salary ranges from Rs 5,200 to Rs 20,200 and a Grade Pay of Rs 1,800.
  • This position is categorized as a General Central Service Group ‘C’ Non-Gazetted and Non-Ministerial role, and it is available in various Ministries/Departments/Offices of the Government of India across different States/Union Territories.
  • As per the guidelines of the 7th pay commission, the in-hand salary for SSC MTS falls within the range of Rs 18,000 to Rs 22,000 per month, depending on the job post and the allocated city.

SSC MTS 2023 Answer Key

SSC will release the SSC MTS Tier 1 Answer Key on the official website approximately one month after the examination. Initially, a provisional answer key for SSC MTS will be made available, which will be followed by the final answer key. It’s important to note that an answer key won’t be released for SSC MTS Tier-2 and Skill tests.

Candidates are required to exclusively download the SSC MTS Answer Key from the official website using their unique registration number and password.
