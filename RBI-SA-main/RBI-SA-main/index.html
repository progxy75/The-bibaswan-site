RBI Assistant Examination 2023: Online Application Form

The beginning of the online registration process for the RBI Assistant Examination 2023 will start once the dates are released by RBI. Interested candidates have the option to submit their application form by visiting the official website. To avoid any last-minute difficulties, candidates are requested to apply for the different posts on or before the specified deadline.

RBI Assistant Examination 2023: Application Fee

Candidates are required to pay an application fee after the completion of the application process. Check the information below to know about the application fee:

  • SC/ST/PWD/EXS. (Intimation Charges): Rs.50/-
  • OBC/General/EWS candidates (Examination fees+ Intimation Charges): Rs 450/-

Note: Staff candidates are exempted from paying examination fees as well as intimation charges.

RBI Assistant Examination 2023: Eligibility Criteria

Candidates need to meet the following criteria in order to appear for the RBI Assistant examination 2023.

a) RBI Assistant Examination 2023: Nationality

  • Indian citizenship.
  • Age between 20 and 28 years.

b) RBI Assistant Examination 2023: Educational Qualification

  • A bachelor’s degree from a recognized college or university in India, with a minimum of 50% marks.
  • Proficiency in computer skills.
  • Language proficiency as per the requirements of the specific state or city.

Note: For SC/ST/PWD categories, there is no requirement for a minimum of 50% marks. Only a graduate degree with a pass class is all required for SC/ST/PwBD candidates.

c) RBI Assistant Examination 2023: Age Limit

A candidate must be between the ages of 20 to 28 years in order to be eligible for the RBI Assistant Exam 2023. Please note that only candidates born between 02/02/1994 and 01/02/2002 (both days inclusive) are eligible to apply. Age relaxation, as per government rules, can be referred to in the table provided below.

RBI Assistant Examination 2023: Exam Pattern

a) RBI Assistant 2023: Prelims Exam Pattern

The initial phase of RBI Assistant Examination is the Preliminary Examination. Check the information below:

  • Prelims Exam comprises three sections: English, Numerical Ability, and Reasoning.
  • There will be a negative marking of 0.25 marks for each incorrect answer.
  • The duration of the RBI Assistant Prelims Exam is 1 hour (60 minutes).
  • The candidate will have 20 minutes for each section.

c) RBI Assistant 2023: Language Proficiency Test

Candidates who qualify the main examination need to undergo language proficiency test (LPT). This test will be administered in the Official/Local Language of the specific state. Those who are not proficient in the Official/Local Language of the state will not be able to continue in the selection process and will be disqualified.

RBI Assistant Examination 2023: Selection Process

The recruitment procedure of RBI Assistant involves three stages: the prelims stage, the main stage, and the language skills assessment.RBI Assistant Prelims and Main Exam have a negative marking of 0.25 marks. It is compulsory to clear all three stages to get selected for RBI Assistant Posts.

RBI Assistant Examination 2023: Admit Card

Candidates who have successfully and accurately filled out the online application form can download their RBI Assistant Admit Examination 2023 Card from the official website. It is important to note that separate admit cards will be issued for each phase of the examination.

RBI Assistant Examination 2023: Result

The RBI Assistant 2023 Result will be initially released for the Prelims Exam, followed by the Mains. The final merit list will be released after the Language Proficiency Test (LPT). Candidates can check the results for each phase as soon as they are officially declared.

RBI Assistant Examination 2023: Salary

An RBI Assistant receives a salary of approximately Rs 40,050/- per month, which includes HRA (House Rent Allowance). In addition to the salary, employees are eligible for various benefits such as DA, expense reimbursement, dispensary facility, loans, advances at concessional interest rates, and gratuity.

Check the below provided information:

The Basic Pay for an RBI Assistant is Rs 20,700/- per month.

The gross salary of an RBI Assistant amounts to Rs 45,050/- per month.

The Reserve Bank of India conducts the RBI Assistant Exam every year to fill openings for assistant positions in the Reserve Bank bank. The exam is divided into three stages: preliminary, mains, and language proficiency.

The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) Assistant examination is a highly sought-after opportunity for individuals who want to work in the banking industry. In order to qualify this tough exam on your first attempt, you must prepare thoroughly, manage your time effectively, and take a strategic approach.

A selected candidate will be required to serve a six-month probationary period before being assigned a permanent position as an RBI Assistant. However, there is a two-year service or bond requirement before being considered for promotion.

An RBI Assistant receives a salary of approximately Rs 40,050/- per month, which includes HRA (House Rent Allowance). In addition to the salary, employees are eligible for various benefits such as DA, expense reimbursement, dispensary facility, loans, advances at concessional interest rates, and gratuity.

The RBI Assistant selection procedure does not include an interview stage. Candidates must qualify a preliminary examination, a main examination, and a Language Proficiency Test in order to get selected for the position of RBI Assistant.
